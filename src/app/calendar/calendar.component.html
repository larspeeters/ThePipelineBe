GAME & GEEK CALENDAR

There’s a lot of different events throughout the year and sometimes it’s really rather difficult to figure out and remember when what happens. That’s why we’ve decided to keep a little calendar consisting of all the Game & Geek related events happening in the Benelux. That way you can always be aware of your favorite event happening!

Events can be related to:
Video Games / Board games / Tabletop games / Comics / TV series / Movies / Books / Cosplay / LARPs / ...

How do I use it?

The link below is an iCal feed that can be integrated into your own calendar. Copy / paste the link below and import the URL to your Google, Apple, Outlook, or other calendar application. Import the URL - don't download the file at the URL - and your calendar will automatically synchronize newly added events.

https://calendar.google.com/calendar/ical/6uiheemqmcfpbknq48m3snkll4%40group.calendar.google.com/public/basic.ics

BUT… YOU’RE MISSING SOME EVENTS?

If you think we’re missing something, please feel free to reach out to us at contact@thepipeline.be or join our discord, and tell us which events we are missing. We will take swift action and make sure the missing event gets added.

Please note that events have to:

  • Be open for everyone to attend
  • Be in or around Belgium
  • Take place in the physical, non-virtual, realm
