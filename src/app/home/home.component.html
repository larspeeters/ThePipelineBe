Community

Using our active Discord server and social groups we are able to provide our community with the necessary media to communicate with eachother.

Events

We try our best to organize, support and attend as many of these events as possible in and around Belgium in order to bring people closer together in real life.

Game nights

We try to organize as many online game sessions as we can by hosting servers where people can enjoy a game together.

Streaming tips

We try to help and support the Belgian streamer scene by boosting their reach, offering them special opportunities through sponsors or partners and organizing specialized streaming events.
